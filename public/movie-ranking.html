The Best Movies According to Det

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

Known for its unique visual style, quirky humor, and masterful storytelling. The film features a stellar ensemble cast and is visually striking with its meticulous design.

The Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Features a unique narrative structure, quirky humor, and profound exploration of relationships. The performances by Jim Carrey and Kate Winslet are exceptional.

Ponyo

Known for its charming storytelling, beautiful animation, whimsical, heartfelt narrative, and a happy ending. It's produced by Studio Ghibli.
